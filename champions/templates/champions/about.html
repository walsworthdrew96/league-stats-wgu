Why we started this website

Our story began with a simple premise: Winning feels good and we wanted others to win more often. While searching for tools and stats for League of Legends, we decided to build our own. This website is for those who want to learn how to win. We provide definitive League of Legends champion stats and enable you to increase your rank in competitive play.
